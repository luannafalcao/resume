About Me

Hi, I'm Luanna Falcão, a UX designer and visual artist with a passion for storytelling, systems thinking, and aesthetic clarity. My career spans over a decade, blending public sector innovation, design research, and digital experimentation across Brazil, Estonia, and the U.S.

I believe thoughtful design emerges from deep listening. My approach is rooted in understanding human behavior, transforming complex needs into accessible and meaningful experiences — whether through interactive prototypes, visual narratives, or minimalist interfaces.

Currently based in Arlington, VA, I divide my time between studying UX, Frontend development and doing independent art projects, exploring symbolic, surreal, and socially reflective themes. My toolbox includes Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud, and a steady curiosity for the intersections between form and function.
